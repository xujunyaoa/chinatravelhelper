Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before downloading.

Is China Travel Helper really free? +
Yes! The core app is 100% free with all essential features: payment setup guide, offline menu translation, emergency SOS, metro maps, and 300+ travel phrases. We plan optional premium features (VIP travel concierge, offline map packs) but the survival kit stays free.
Does the app work without internet? +
Absolutely. Key features are designed to work offline from the moment you land: menu translation (5,000+ dish names), travel phrases with audio, metro maps, emergency cards, and cultural tips. Route planning and real-time weather need internet.
Which languages does the app support? +
The app interface is available in English, Japanese (日本語), Korean (한국어), and Chinese (中文). Translation features support text and camera input across multiple languages.
Can I really use my foreign credit card for WeChat Pay? +
Yes! Since 2024, WeChat Pay and Alipay both support linking international Visa, Mastercard, and JCB cards. Our step-by-step guide shows the exact screens with screenshots, updated to match the latest app versions. You can pay at 90%+ of merchants in China this way.
When is the iOS version coming? +
We're actively developing the iOS version. Join our email list to be notified when it launches on the App Store. In the meantime, all web guides at this site work on any device.
How is my data handled? +
We take privacy seriously. Your personal data (allergies, emergency contacts, trip details) stays on your device. We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Read our full Privacy Policy.
