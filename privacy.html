Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

China Travel Helper ("we", "our", or "us") operates the China Travel Helper mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication credentials via Firebase Authentication.

Usage Data: We may collect information about how you interact with the App, including feature usage, crash logs, and performance data.

Location Data: With your explicit permission, we may access your device location to provide map, weather, and nearby services features. You can disable this at any time in your device settings.

Camera Access: We access your camera solely for the menu translation feature. Images are processed on-device for offline translation and are not uploaded to our servers unless you choose to use cloud translation.

Hotel Bookings: When you book hotels through our affiliate partners (Trip.com, Booking.com), you are redirected to their platforms. We do not collect or store your payment information. Please refer to their respective privacy policies.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's core features
  • To improve user experience based on usage analytics
  • To communicate with you about updates and support
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Third-Party Services

Our App integrates with the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google LLC)
  • Alibaba Cloud Translation & OCR
  • AMap (AutoNavi) Maps
  • OpenWeatherMap
  • Trip.com & Booking.com (affiliate bookings)

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers using industry-standard encryption. We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. Offline data (phrasebooks, cached translations) is stored locally on your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the App.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can request data deletion by contacting us at support@chinatravelhelper.com. If you are in the EU/EEA, you have additional rights under GDPR. If you are in China, you have rights under the Personal Information Protection Law (PIPL).

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the App and on this page.
